I enjoy doing a wide variety of things.

Running

Since middle school I have always been a runner at heart. While, I have tried other sports such as football, basketball, and baseball, I have always came back to the thing they all have in common. I started with cross country and quickly realized I was more of a sprinter.My passion for it took me into track & field, where I focused on the 400m. Running is so much more than going out and moving your legs, it is honestly a lot nerdier than it seems. You have to focus on your biomechanics, what you put into your body, and how you take care of yourself. Without those things you will not get to where you want to be as a runner.

SpikeBall

It is a sport that has been up and coming in the past few years but I have played SpikeBall for around 6 years now. I met a good group of friends doing it and I have even traveled for tournaments competing with international teams as well as colleges. If you are not familiar with Spikeball think of it like volleyball but instead of passing the ball over a net you bounce it off a central net placed on the ground. In 2022 I was ranked #55 out of 100 Spikeball Players in the state of Texas.

Nature

The outdoors wasn't always something that I loved. I had to be exposed to it. Growing up, my family has taken multiple vacations to Colorado to get out of our normal scene in the city. I have ziplined over the mountains, white water rafted, hiked through waterfalls, and just simply allowed myself to exist in nature. Ive also spent a week in a yurt by the ocean in oregon. Some of my favorite outdoor activities are hiking, kyaking, and rock climbing.

Gaming

Before I could remember I have always loved gaming. I grew up playig Fifa and Skyrim with my uncle. Then, it led to my dad and I finding a common interest when Destiny The Game released. I think what really got me into gaming is the community that it can bring between people. It is hard to understand unless you have experienced it. To this day my dad and I still play Destiny together and we even have our old characters on the Playstation from since I was a kid. Believe it or not this has been something that I have even made a good bit of money off it.
